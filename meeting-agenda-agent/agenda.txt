Meeting Agenda (2025-12-30)
=============================================

Title: Q1 Product Planning
Objective: Decide priorities for Q1 roadmap and align teams
Duration: 60 minutes

1. Introduction and Meeting Objectives (5 min)
   Owner: Product Manager
   Outcome: Clear understanding of meeting goals and agenda

2. Review Proposed Features for Q1 (15 min)
   Owner: Product Manager
   Outcome: All participants understand the feature options

3. Discussion on Feature Priorities (20 min)
   Owner: All Participants
   Outcome: Consensus on the importance and impact of each feature

4. Decision: Select Top 3 Features for Q1 Roadmap (15 min)
   Owner: All Participants
   Outcome: Agreement on top 3 features to prioritize (Decision Point)

5. Alignment on Next Steps and Team Responsibilities (5 min)
   Owner: Product Manager
   Outcome: Clear action items and team alignment
